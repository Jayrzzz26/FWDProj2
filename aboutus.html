Our Story

What got us started?

Destravel was founded back in 2020 by our founder, John Lenett, who was a vivid traveler who enjoyed exploring the different culture and enviroment each country has to.

John was always up for an ADEVENTURE even in his early days as a student, but due to budget constraints, his adventure were restricted to his budget and this did not sit right with him

And thus, his journey began to find ways to MAXIMIZE his adventure given the budget he had or MINIMIZE his output while still having the full experience of his adeventures.

Why Destravel?

With the creation of Destravel, our sole purpose is to ENABLE like minded travelers to have an easy way of maximising both their budget and travel experience

Destravel is your one stop place to view the best travel adventure places and activities at a very LOW COST as well as your accomodation and transportation.

Let us save you the hussle and worry of not being able to save enough or miss out on the adventure of a LIFETIME!
